Study Space Reviews

Some of my favorite study spots:

Interior Highlight Drink
High ceilings, simple colors Creative seating Rose Matcha Latte

Ogawa Coffee; Milk St, Boston MA

Rose matcha latte is sophisticatedly balanced, and the aroma of sprinkled rose petals accompanies every sip. Simple interior, windows stretching from the floor to high ceilings, ample types of seating, and soft classical music in the background. If its address isn’t clue enough that Ogawa is the perfect study cafe, this is your sign.

Render Coffee; Columbus Ave, Boston MA

Very sweet chai lattes. The main attraction is the sunroom at the back, which fills up quickly. Feel surrounded by the outdoors while cozily doing work indoors. I’d like to return when it’s rainy.
