Captain for eCitadel Open Defense Competition

05-19-2024

Quick four hour competition this past weekend! I was the captain for our team of 4.

The 2024 eCitadel Open had 4 systems: Windows Server 2016, Windows Server 2022, Linux Mint 21, and Alma Linux OS 9 which I was on. The competition is scored based on web challenges, maintaining critical services, and patching misconfigurations and vulnerabilities.

Overall, we placed 43rd out of 159 teams. We didn’t take it too intensely, just enjoyed practicing security, forensic, configuration, and system administration on vulnerable systems.
