Starting CTF Club at Northeastern

2023-09-01

This semester, myself and a talented group of fellow board members are starting a CTF club at Northeastern. While there is already a cybersecurity club on campus, they largely focus on social and networking events. Myself and others felt a need to have a technically-focused club for students of all experience levels to come and learn how to hack, participate in CTFs, and see how cybersecurity is applied in simulated real-world scenarios.

As a co-founder and VP, I have been crafting diverse CTF challenges for our upcoming competitions and general meetings. During workshops, we will also provide technical guidance to members. I have also been leading club operations, as my last two years on the board of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) has given me plenty of experience in the elements of running an organization. This includes event planning, social media presence and marketing, interfacing with the college, and initiatives to increase member engagement. I guide our weekly board meetings, in which I ensure action items are properly distributed and align with the larger goals of our organization.

I’m excited to build up this club into an established presence on campus!
