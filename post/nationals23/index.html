National CCDC Competition

2023-05-01

This year, Northeastern’s CCDC team made it to the national competition in Texas, and we ended up scoring 5th place!

This was definitely the most simultaneously stressful and fun cybersecurity experience I’ve ever had. We were given the diagram of the network infrastructure only an hour before the start of the competition, and we scrambled to establish a game plan. Immediately began to secure our machines, which were riddled with misconfigurations and vulnerabilities. I really enjoyed the realism of the competition, and some much needed comic relief came with every ring of our company phone which we answered in our best customer-service voice. The red team had quickly and easily established persistence within the first hour of the competition, but we did our best to remediate our systems and block further attacks.

The adrenaline made the time pass quickly, and after two days of competition, we scored 5th in the nation!
